Studio Luzano Koulu

Small, beginner-friendly observation drawing classes in my studio in Kruununhaka, Helsinki. We focus on seeing like an artist: clear shapes, confident marks, and calm, focused looking.

About the class

Studio Luzano Koulu – Observation Drawing Basics is for anyone who wants to learn how to really see what’s in front of them and translate it onto paper. No previous experience is needed; curiosity and patience are enough.

We work with still-life setups, simple objects and everyday scenes. The focus is on building confidence, not perfection: slow looking, clear shapes, and relaxed mark-making.

What you’ll learn

  • How to find clear, simple shapes in complex subjects.
  • Basic proportion and measuring by eye.
  • Understanding light and shadow to suggest form.
  • Line quality and relaxed, confident mark-making.
  • Building a small, sustainable drawing practice at home.
